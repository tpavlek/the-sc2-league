The League of Extraordinary SC2 Organizers is a group of passionate, hard-working organizers of events in Starcraft 2 who are committed to bringing the best possible events to the wider community. We collaborate and plan in a group setting to reduce conflicts and burnout and ensure the success of the whole community is always at the forefront.

Join the League

Certain aspects of organizing SC2 events require privacy in discussion. Therefore, membership to the private mailing list is restricted. If you're an organizer of SC2 events and would like to participate in the discussion you can apply to join the private mailing list here.

Get Involved

Even if you're not an organizer, there's still plenty to do!

If you're looking to help out, there's lots you can contribute to! We're not superheroes and we can't do it all!

The first thing you should do is join our public mailing list, that will keep you alerted of all the new events that are ready to be announced. If we're looking for artists, casters, translators, advertisers or any other help, we'll be sure to ask on this mailing list.

Join the public list

Other things you can do

  • Post about upcoming events on Reddit/Teamliquid
  • Follow organizers on Social Media
  • Tune in to streams when they're on
  • Encourage your favourite organizers to join the league
  • Donate your time or money
  • Most importantly: Speak positively about the events organized for the community
